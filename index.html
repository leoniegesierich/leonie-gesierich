About me

Between Business and the Stage

Hi, I'm Leonie – 22 years old, raised near Berlin, and endlessly fascinated by what happens between the first steps of organising a concert and the moment an artist steps on stage and a crowd holds its breath.

At TH Wildau I study European Business Management, with a Future in Marketing in mind and striving for a job in the Music Industry. My goal: to one day work in music marketing.

When I'm not studying, I'm most likely at a concert — sometimes in another city, sometimes across the country. For the right show, I'll always find a way there.
